# Privacy Policy

EcoSuds only asks for the information needed to answer product questions, confirm orders and arrange delivery. This may include your name, phone number, delivery city, delivery address, product choices and message history shared through WhatsApp, email or social media.

Customer details are used for order confirmation, support, delivery coordination and service improvement. EcoSuds does not sell customer information.

Messages sent through WhatsApp, email, Instagram or Facebook are also handled by those platforms under their own privacy practices. Customers should avoid sending sensitive payment details in open chat unless specifically requested through a safe, agreed method.

Customers can request correction or removal of their contact details by messaging EcoSuds through the contact options on this website.
