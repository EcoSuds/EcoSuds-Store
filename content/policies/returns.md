# Refund & Exchange Policy

EcoSuds handles refund and exchange requests through WhatsApp support. Because soaps, bath products and aroma products are personal-care items, opened or used products cannot normally be returned.

If a product arrives damaged, incorrect or missing from the order, customers should contact EcoSuds as soon as possible with the order details and clear photos. EcoSuds will review the issue and confirm the best solution, which may include replacement, exchange, store credit or another fair resolution.

Final eligibility depends on product condition, delivery record and the details shared by the customer. Delivery charges may apply where a return or exchange requires additional shipping.
