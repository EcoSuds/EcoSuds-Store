# Shipping Policy

EcoSuds confirms orders directly through WhatsApp before dispatch. Delivery availability, delivery charges, expected timing and payment method are shared with the customer before an order is finalized.

Customers should provide the correct name, phone number, delivery city and complete address to avoid delays. If an address is incomplete or a courier cannot reach the customer, EcoSuds may request updated details before dispatching or re-sending an order.

Product availability may vary by batch. When a selected item is unavailable, EcoSuds will suggest a replacement, adjusted bundle or revised dispatch time before confirming the order.
