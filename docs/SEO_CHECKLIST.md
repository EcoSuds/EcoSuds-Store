# SEO Checklist

- Set `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` to the live production domain.
- Keep each product title descriptive and unique.
- Keep product slugs short, lowercase and stable.
- Add original product photos to replace temporary remote category/product placeholders when available.
- Keep category pages linked from Home, EcoSuds and Store pages.
- Update `content/site.json` keywords as the product range grows.
- Review policy pages when delivery areas, payment methods or support processes change.
- Submit `/sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console after deployment.
